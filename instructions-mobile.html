Instructions

How do you enter the FionCash Private Telegram Group?

  • Step 1: Go to https://t.me/fioncash and enter the FionCash Lobby in Telegram
  • Step 2: Click on the Tap to verify button, you will be redirected to the Safeguard bot
  • Step 3: Click on the Verify button, then on the Start button to start the verification
  • Step 4: Click on Click here and the Telegram Group temporary link will appear
  • Step 5: Click on the temporary Telegram link to enter the FionCash Private Group

We follow this process to protect ourselves from spam accounts.
If you need any help, please send an email to fioncash@outlook.com
